Introduction

Hello! My name is Roger Kinney, and im a 34 year old male from Sacramento Ca. I started my life in the military, visited many countries such as Australia, Iraq, Egypt, Germany, Ireland and even Kuwait. I have a bachelors in criminal justice and worked in law enforcement for about 5 years. I always get asked why do I want to switch careers? Work life balance is important, and as a Iraq veteran suffering with severe PTSD, I found that my career choice wasnt the best. I took a lot of interest and actually coded when I was in highschool. I even managed to make a few pages about the origional LOTR series. I want a field that challenges me and takes me out of my comfort zone so, software dev seems like an idealistic choice.

University of Davis Ca Coding Academy

The UC Davis bootcamp taught me both up to date, widely used programming languages coupled with respective framework all resulting in a successful entry level full stack developer skillset. The curriculum is heavily project-based, and required me to immediately put fast paced learned concepts into practice. I was capable of building fully functional web-apps, do data analysis and use real dev tools just weeks into my programs. I worked countless hours outside of the program to improve my skillset with having none from day one. A self starter by nature, coupled with professional partners on projects, I found myself able to capitalize on instruction and the corrective criticism on a functioning team.

Marine Veteran

Marines take pride in their ability to be professional, hardworking and dilligent members in society. As a Iraq veteran, I was able to product project based work while in the harshest of enviorments. I worked supply and logistics while deployed conducting objective oriented combat operations in the Al Anbar Province.
